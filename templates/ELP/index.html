About us

UJUZISETI is a cofounded platform between Mzumbe University, Vlir-uos 4SITe programme, TRIAS, DonBosco Tanzania, Smartcore Enterprise and TCCIA, As an exceptional entrepreneurship skills platform. We believe that reliable skills are the strongest ladder that take both individual person and business enterprise to the climax point of growth and development. From this perspective, UJUZISETI is committed to deliver extraordinary entrepreneurship courses that are up-to-date and reliable ones to the target audiences. The platform aims at tremendous transformation in the field of entrepreneurship to both practitioners and entrepreneurship skills seekers. We structure your entrepreneurship skills for your brightest future growth and development.
